Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best time to visit AB's Homestay in Coorg?
The best time is October-March when the weather is pleasant with clear skies perfect for trekking & sightseeing. Monsoon (June-September) is magical for misty landscapes but roads can be slippery. Summer (April-May) is hot but less crowded.
Do you provide airport pickup/drop from Bangalore or Mangalore?
Yes! We can arrange transportation from Bangalore (270 km, ~5 hours) or Mangalore airport (180 km, ~4 hours). Mention this when booking & we'll provide custom quotes. Our guests love the scenic drive through the ghats!
Is there WiFi & mobile network at the homestay?
Absolutely! Free high-speed WiFi available in all rooms & common areas. Mobile network (Jio, Airtel, Vodafone) is also strong. Come disconnect from the city noise but stay connected if you need to!
What meals are included? Are there vegetarian/dietary options?
Complimentary breakfast is included with every stay. Lunch & dinner are available at nominal rates. We specialize in authentic Coorgi cuisine, but offer vegetarian, vegan, & gluten-free options. Just inform us during booking!
Can I cancel my booking? What's your cancellation policy?
Free cancellation up to 7 days before arrival. 50% refund for cancellations 3-7 days prior. No refund for cancellations within 3 days. Rescheduling to different dates is always welcome!
Is the homestay suitable for families with kids & elderly guests?
Definitely! Our Family Suite is spacious & perfect for kids. The property is peaceful & safe with beautiful garden space. Elderly guests appreciate the serene environment & moderate altitude (1200m). Ground floor rooms available on request.
